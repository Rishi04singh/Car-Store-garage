Our Story

Established in 2010, Rishi Garage has been a trusted name in automotive care and service. From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a fully equipped, modern garage offering everything from basic maintenance to advanced engine diagnostics. Our mission is simple — to provide honest, high-quality work that keeps your vehicle safe, efficient, and road-ready.

With over a decade of experience, our team of skilled professionals combines technology with craftsmanship to ensure every customer drives away satisfied.
